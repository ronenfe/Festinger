Manual

  1. The program will show you the history of your calls in accordance to the built in call logs, so if you clear the built in call logs, it will be cleared from Historia too.
  2. To sort by column, simply tap the column header you want to sort by, you can sort in ascending order or descending order, and a little arrow near the column header will indicate that.
  3. To copy a number or a call to the clipboard, simply tap and hold the call and choose the appropriate option.
